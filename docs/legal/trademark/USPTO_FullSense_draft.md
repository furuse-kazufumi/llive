## 8. Filing process

  1. Conduct TESS search at <https://www.uspto.gov/trademarks/search>
  2. Create account on USPTO Trademark Center
  3. Designate US attorney (required for non-US applicants)
  4. Complete TEAS form (Plus or Standard)
  5. Upload specimens
  6. Pay filing fee
  7. Receive serial number (within hours)
  8. Examination phase (~6-9 months)
  9. Respond to Office Actions if any
  10. Publication for opposition (30 days)
  11. Registration (if no opposition)
